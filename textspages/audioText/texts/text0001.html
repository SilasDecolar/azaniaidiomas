Deer journal

Monday January 8

Dear diary, Every Monday, it's the same thing. My alarm goes off at six thirty, I go back to sleep for fifteen more minutes, and I get up around six forty-five. Then I take my shower to finish waking up.

After, I go into the kitchen and drink my coffee. Sometimes, like this morning, I have croissants leftover from the weekend, otherwise I eat buttered toats. I am not very hungry in the morning, even though I know it's the most important meal of the day. I also take advantage of breakfast to read the news online.

Usually that's when I realize I'm late! I run to my room, and hurry to get dressed and put on makeup, then I'm going to take the subway!

Maybe I should wake up a little earlier to avoid that! Yes, dear Diary, it's decided, next Monday, I will set my alarm earlier!
